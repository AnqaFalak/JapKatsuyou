Inflection::conjugate Gives complicated conjugation forms, such as polite positive past form (tense)

It gives complex conjugation forms, using the basic stems, and adding suffixes according to parameters: type , form , polite , and affirmative .

Parameters
verbThe verb in dictionary form (u-form), eg. 食べる, 飲む, 行く, 来る, etc.
typeType of the verb: v1, v5u, etc. (See EdictType)
formComplex form (tense) which we want to conjugate to, such as: present, past, conditional, etc. (See CForm)
politeThe language can be polite or plain, ans thus verb conjugation variate according to these two. (See Politeness)
affirmativeThe sentence can be affirmative (positive) or negative. (See Polarity)
Returns
The complex verb conjugation

Inflection::katsuyou Gives basic conjugation forms, as thought in Japanese schools.

It gives basic conjugation forms (or basic stems), those basic forms can be used to form complex conjugation forms by adding suffixes and auxilary verbs.
